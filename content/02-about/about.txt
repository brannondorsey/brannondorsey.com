Title: About
----
Text: <p class="text">Brannon Dorsey is an Art and Technology student at the School of The Art Institute of Chicago. His artistic practice represents a diverse scope of mediums ranging creative code, programming, and new media art to sculpture, photography, and film. His work has shown in New York City, Chicago, Miami, Washington D.C., Philidelphia , and his hometown, Richmond, VA. He has shown in group exhibitions at the Virginia Museum of Fine Arts, the Miami Art Museum, the John F. Kennedy Center for the Performing Arts, and the Smithsonian's American Art Museum.</p><p class="text">
